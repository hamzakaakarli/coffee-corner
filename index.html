About Me

I’m a long-time coffee shop enthusiast who’s spent years exploring cafés across the U.S. and abroad. I treat cafés as a true third space — part study hall, part social hub — where I learn, read, and meet friends. I’m drawn to vibrant atmospheres that spark conversation and help me stay productive, and I review each spot with both comfort and craft in mind.

Ranking Criteria

Each coffee shop is visited multiple times and evaluated over time. My goal is to capture both first impressions and consistency across visits.

  • Coffee taste
  • Price of coffee, drinks, and food
  • Seating comfort
  • Cleanliness
  • Decor and vibes
